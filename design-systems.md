## Global Rules

### Platform Identity
**QuickFare** is a premium Islamic ebook streaming platform. Every cover must feel like it belongs to a curated, world-class digital library. The visual language must honor Islamic aesthetic traditions while delivering contemporary digital polish.

### Standard Cover Dimensions
- **Aspect Ratio**: 2:3 (portrait)
- **Base Resolution**: 1000 × 1500 px
- **Production Resolution**: 2000 × 3000 px (2× for retina displays)
- **Safe Area Margin**: 80 px from all edges (920 × 1340 px content zone)
- **Text Safe Zone**: 120 px from top and bottom edges; 100 px from left and right edges

### QuickFare Branding Placement
- **Logo**: QuickFare wordmark or monogram appears on every cover
- **Position**: Bottom-right corner, 60 px from right edge, 60 px from bottom edge
- **Size**: 120–160 px wide (proportional to cover width)
- **Color Rule**: Logo uses white `#FFFFFF` with 80% opacity on dark backgrounds; uses `#0A1628` with 70% opacity on light backgrounds
- **Alternative**: Subtle embossed monogram in bottom-right when the cover design is highly ornamental

### Minimum Text Contrast Ratio
- **WCAG AA Standard**: Minimum 4.5:1 for body/subtitle text; 3:1 for large title text (18pt+ equivalent at cover scale)
- **Text Shadow/Glow**: Required when text overlays directly on photographic or patterned backgrounds
- **Text Bar Fallback**: Semi-transparent background bar (black or white at 20–40% opacity) behind text when contrast cannot be achieved otherwise

### Urdu Text Handling
- **Font**: Noto Nastaliq Urdu (primary); Jameel Noori Nastaleeq (alternative for more traditional feel)
- **Direction**: Right-to-left (RTL) rendering mandatory
- **Line Height**: 1.8× minimum (Nastaliq ascenders/descenders require generous vertical spacing)
- **Fallback**: If Urdu title exceeds 3 lines, scale down to 0.85× or abbreviate with ellipsis
- **Diacritic Preservation**: All Arabic diacritics (tashkeel) must render clearly; do not compress line height to the point of overlap

### Cover Anatomy (Universal)
```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐  ← 120 px top safe margin
│      [DECORATIVE ZONE]          │
│                                 │
│    ┌─────────────────────┐      │  ← Upper third focal zone
│    │   TITLE (Primary)   │      │
│    │   URDU TITLE        │      │
│    └─────────────────────┘      │
│                                 │
│      [VISUAL CENTERPIECE]       │  ← Middle focal zone (60% area)
│                                 │
│    ┌─────────────────────┐      │
│    │   SUBTITLE / AUTHOR│      │  ← Lower third info zone
│    │   CATEGORY BADGE    │      │
│    └─────────────────────┘      │
│                           [Q]   │  ← QuickFare logo (bottom-right)
└─────────────────────────────────┘  ← 120 px bottom safe margin
       ↑ 100 px side safe margins ↑
```

### Category Badge System
- Each cover includes a small category identifier badge in the bottom-left or top-left corner
- **Badge Style**: Pill-shaped or rectangular with 4 px corner radius
- **Badge Color**: Uses the category's accent color at 90% opacity with white text
- **Badge Text**: English category abbreviation (e.g., "HAJJ", "QURAN", "FIQH")

Hajj & Ziyarat covers evoke the spiritual elevation of sacred pilgrimage. The visual language channels the Kaaba's solemn geometry, the warmth of Arabian sand at dawn, and the golden light of divine proximity. Every cover should feel like a doorway to the holiest journey in Islam—reverent, luminous, and magnetically inviting.

- **Overlay Strategy**: Titles over patterned backgrounds must use a subtle dark gradient overlay from top (0–40% of height) fading to transparent by mid-point. Alternatively, a semi-transparent bar (`#0A1628` at 30% opacity, 12 px corner radius) behind text.

   - AI Prompt: *"Islamic geometric pattern interpreted as decorative plate or serving tray design, olive green and saffron gold, 6-fold symmetry, suggesting traditional ceramic or metalware, flat 2D decorative, cultural and elegant, isolated"*
   - Usage: Central decorative element; 250–400 px diameter; or background at 25% opacity

6. **Herb and Leaf Scatter**
   - AI Prompt: *"Scattered fresh herb leaves—mint, parsley, and olive leaves, cardamom green and olive tones, casual organic distribution suggesting fresh preparation, some whole some torn, appetizing and natural, flat illustration with subtle shadow, cream background"*
   - Usage: Corner decoration at 60–80% opacity; framing text zones organically

### Composition Rules

- **Text Placement Zones**:
  - Primary Title Zone: Upper third, centered or slightly above, 120–400 px from top
  - Urdu Title: Below English title, centered
  - Subtitle/Author: Lower third, centered, often within or below wreath
  - Food Elements: Organic placement—spices at bottom, steam rising, wreath framing center

- **Safe Margins**: 120 px top/bottom; 100 px sides
- **Focal Point Rules**: Warm centered composition. The olive wreath or geometric plate often forms a central circular frame. Food-photography inspired negative space—plenty of cream background for the eye to rest. Organic shapes dominate; no sharp angles.
- **Overlay Strategy**: Soft warm overlays only. Text over spice textures needs a cream gradient bar (`#FFF8E7` at 70% opacity, 16 px radius, 20 px padding). Steam and wisps can flow over text edges. The overall mood is inviting and uncluttered.
