### Quality Standards
- Hollywood movie poster level — think Marvel, Christopher Nolan, or Oscar poster quality

- Cinematic depth of field, dramatic lighting, atmospheric haze
- 2:3 ratio, 2K resolution, JPG
- Each cover must feel like a unique commissioned artwork worth $100+

- **Travel**: Sweeping vistas, dramatic skies, sense of journey

- **Science**: Cosmic wonder, clean compositions, dramatic scale
- **Psychology**: Introspective, brain/mind visuals, moody lighting
- **Business**: Bold, aspirational, success imagery
- **Food**: Rich, warm, appetizing with golden light
- **Sports**: Dynamic energy, motion, power
- **Kids**: Warm, magical, inviting but still premium
- **Spirituality**: Ethereal, peaceful, divine light
